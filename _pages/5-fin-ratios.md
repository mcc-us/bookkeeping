3 Common Financial Ratios   
 
1. Current Ratio = Current Assets / Current Liabilities   
   
   - *Ratio > 1.5 is good*   

2. Debt Ratio = Total Liabilities / Total Assets 
   
   - *Present as %*    

3. Equity Ratio = Total S.E. / Total Assets   
   
   - *Present as %*   
   - Equity ratio = 1 - Debt ratio
