## 2. Equity <- Common Shares + Retained Earnings - Dividends - Drawings

## 3. Trial Balance Sheet

Order accounts from top to bottom with ALORE

## 4. Balance Sheet <- A = L + SE  

4 Columns: **Asset Act, Amount, (L+E)Act, Amount**
